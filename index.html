Common Questions

House Clearance FAQ

How much does a house clearance cost in Newcastle?

Costs vary based on property size and volume of items. We offer free, no-obligation quotes and we also pay you for items of value, which can significantly reduce your overall cost. Call us on 07911 123 456 for an instant estimate.

Do you pay for items during a clearance?

Yes — unlike many clearance companies, we assess your items and pay fair prices for anything of value: furniture, antiques, appliances, collectables and more. Cash paid on the day.

How quickly can you carry out a house clearance?

Most clearances can be booked for same day or next day. We respond to all enquiries the same day and offer flexible scheduling to suit your timeline and property situation.

Do you cover areas outside Newcastle?

While we are based in Newcastle upon Tyne (NE5 2NE), Clear Home UK provides house clearance services across the whole of the United Kingdom — no location is too far.

Can you help with a bereavement clearance?

Absolutely. We approach every bereavement clearance with sensitivity, discretion and care. We understand how difficult this time can be and work at a pace that suits you.

Do you dispose of items responsibly?

Yes — we recycle, donate to charity, and responsibly dispose of everything we remove. We aim to divert as much waste from landfill as possible, keeping Newcastle and the UK cleaner.
